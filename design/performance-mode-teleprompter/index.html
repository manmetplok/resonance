Performance Mode — live chord teleprompter

Design prototype for ba epic #11. A full-screen, distraction-free view that follows the transport during a take: a huge current chord with a playable fingering diagram, a beat / count-in cue, and a next-chords preview. Readable from across the room. Built strictly on the Resonance theme tokens, the chord-box convention from chord_sheet_pdf.rs, and the fretboard voicing model. Four states shown.
